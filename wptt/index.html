Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Is this plugin legal to use?

Yes, the plugin itself is completely legal. However, you are responsible for ensuring that the content shared on your tracker complies with copyright laws in your jurisdiction.

What are the server requirements?

WordPress 5.0+, PHP 7.4+, MySQL 5.6+, and at least 128MB PHP memory limit. We recommend a VPS or dedicated server for optimal performance.

Do you provide installation support?

Yes! All plans include installation support. Our team can help you set up the plugin and configure it according to your needs.

Can I customize the design?

Absolutely! The plugin includes multiple templates and supports custom CSS. You can also override templates in your theme for complete control.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the plugin, contact us for a full refund.

How do updates work?

Updates are delivered through your WordPress dashboard, just like any other plugin. You'll receive notifications when new versions are available.
